How it Works

Here at Breez Cookiez we know two things: 1. How to make good cookies 2. Everyone loves fresh cookies Our goal is to provide you with a wide selection of cookies baked fresh and delivered to your door. We always bake your cookies the same day they are delivered. In order to get you the freshest cookies, we ask that you book your delivery slot at least five days in advance. We believe that variety is the spice of life, so you get to choose exactly what kind of cookie you want every time.
We know the best cookies are the ones that are still warm straight from the oven, so we include uniqe instructions for each type of cookie so that you can warm your cookies at home. We accept one time orders, but if you love cookies as much as we do, then you will want to sign up for one of our Cookie Club subscriptions! As a part of the Cookie Club, you will get exclusive offers on our newest cookie creations and many special discounts. Take a look at our Cookie Club options to see which one is right for you!
